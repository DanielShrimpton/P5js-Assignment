Example page for planet.js

Use the sliders to adjust certain values. The colour mode is RGB and the colour of the planet takes the shown values (left being 0 and right being 255). The orbit ring takes 255-red, green and 255-blue to make it different to the planet and the orbiting planets take 255- all values to, again, be different from the other two.
