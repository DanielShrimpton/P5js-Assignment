Example page for planet.js

Use the sliders to adjust certain values. The colour mode is RGB and the colour of the planet takes the shown values (left being 0 and right being 255). The orbit ring takes 255-red, green and 255-blue to make it different to the planet and the orbiting planets take 255- all values to, again, be different from the other two.

The slider that changes the orbit changes the angle of the camera around the planet so can give a different perspective to view it from. From left to right it is one full rotation.

The slider for "size of orbit" changes the size of the orbital ring and how far out the little planets will orbit.

The slider for "speed of orbit" is a multiplier value from zero to 5 which will multiply the value of the speed in the sin and cos calculation to adjust its apparent speed accordingly.

Click to create a new planet and press any key to remove them all. The reset button also removes all planets.

The checkbox will make use of the optional p5.renderer and draw it onto a rotating cube.
